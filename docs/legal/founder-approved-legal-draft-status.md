# Founder-Approved Legal Draft Status

**Purpose:** Clarify what the current legal documents are and how they may be used during internal testing.

## Current Position

The repository contains founder-approved operational drafts for:

- Terms of service.
- Privacy policy.
- Worker agreement.
- Customer liability terms.

These drafts are suitable for internal product shaping, prototype testing, and discussion with providers or counsel. They are not a substitute for formal legal advice if the company starts paid beta, hires/contracts workers, buys insurance, or handles real disputes.

## Founder Decision

The founder authorizes continued build and internal testing using the current drafts as working policy.
