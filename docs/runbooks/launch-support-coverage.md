# Launch Support Coverage

## Coverage Model

- Closed beta: founder/operator coverage during all scheduled visit windows.
- Open beta: named primary and backup operator every day.
- Public launch: support coverage published before marketing push.

## Channels

- In-app subscriber support.
- Worker issue reporting.
- Operator callback for urgent cases.
- Manual phone fallback for provider or app outage.

## Staffing Rule

Pause growth if support work exceeds one operator-hour per 30 active subscribers per week or if urgent issues cannot be acknowledged within 15 minutes during visit windows.

## Daily Checklist

- Review Beta/Open Beta metrics.
- Review unresolved disputes and worker issues.
- Review payment failures and reconciliation.
- Review no-shows and route gaps.
- Confirm next-day worker capacity.

## Handoff

Every handoff must include:

- active incidents;
- open disputes;
- unresolved payment issues;
- unavailable workers;
- next-day high-risk visits.
