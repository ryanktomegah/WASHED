# Open Beta Expansion Runbook

## Entry Criteria

- Closed beta NPS is at least 40 in the final two weeks.
- Worker satisfaction is at least 4/5.
- Visit completion is at least 95%.
- Payment success is at least 98% or a provider remediation plan is approved.
- No Sev-1 bug is open.

## Cohort Target

- 200 subscribers.
- 25 active workers.
- Keep route density by service cell; do not add neighborhoods that break completion or support coverage.

## Expansion Steps

1. Add 5 workers and verify first-route readiness.
2. Add 40 subscribers and hold for one week.
3. Review beta metrics, support load, route duration, and payment success.
4. Repeat until 200 subscribers or until a stop condition is hit.

## Stop Conditions

- Completion below 95% for seven days.
- Support load exceeds planned operator coverage.
- Payment failure spike without provider confirmation.
- Worker payout failure above 2%.
- Safety incident unresolved.
