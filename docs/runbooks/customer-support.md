# Customer Support Runbook

## Triage

1. Search subscriber by phone in the Operator Console Support view.
2. Confirm subscription status, next visit, recent visits, payments, support credits, disputes, and worker assignment.
3. Classify the request: scheduling, payment, worker swap, quality issue, damage/loss, safety, or account access.

## Scheduling

- For simple changes, use skip, reschedule, tier switch, or cancellation flows.
- If a worker cannot serve the new slot, move the case to matching/live ops.
- Confirm the next visit date and time window in French before closing.

## Payment

- Check payment history and latest provider status.
- For failed payment, ask the subscriber to retry after confirming mobile-money balance and phone number.
- For duplicate or disputed charge, create an operator note and escalate to Payments before issuing refunds.

## Quality Or Damage

- Open or create a dispute.
- Collect description, photos, visit ID, worker statement, and timeline.
- Do not promise refund amount until an operator decision is recorded.

## Worker Swap

- Use swap request flow.
- Confirm the reason and urgency.
- Tell the subscriber the existing worker remains assigned until operations approves a replacement.

## Closeout

- Record the outcome in the operator note or dispute resolution.
- Add support credit only when the reason is clear.
- Reopen if the subscriber replies with new evidence.
