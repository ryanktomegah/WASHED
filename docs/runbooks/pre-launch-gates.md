# Pre-Launch Gates

## Product And Operations

- Closed beta exit criteria passed.
- Open beta exit criteria passed.
- Support coverage live.
- Incident escalation runbook tested.
- Provider-failure tabletop executed in staging.

## Legal And Finance

- ToS, privacy policy, worker agreement, and liability terms counsel-approved in French.
- Worker classification guidance received.
- Trademark/name check complete.
- Payment provider contract and settlement process approved.
- Public-launch budget approved.

## Technical

- Production route guards enabled.
- Provider credentials stored outside code.
- Payment, OTP, push, object storage, and Sentry readiness endpoints clean.
- Load test at launch scale passes.
- Status page and rollback process live.
- External security/accessibility review approved or launch-blocking issues closed.

## Go / No-Go

Launch only when every gate is either passed or explicitly accepted in writing by the founder with a named owner and mitigation date.
