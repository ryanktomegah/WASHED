# Worker Onboarding Materials

## Pipeline

1. Application received.
2. CNI uploaded.
3. References called.
4. Police clearance received when required.
5. Training scheduled.
6. Uniform issued.
7. First route assigned.

## Training Checklist

- Explain Washed service promise and punctuality expectations.
- Practice worker app login, route view, check-in, photo capture, check-out, issue reporting, and earnings screen.
- Verify the worker can use GPS and camera on the real device.
- Explain when to call operations: unsafe home, missing client, locked gate, damaged item, illness, transport problem, payment question.
- Review privacy rules: do not share addresses, photos, phone numbers, or client details.

## First Route Readiness

- Worker status is active.
- Service neighborhoods are correct.
- Max active subscriptions is set.
- Worker has uniform and support contact.
- Operator has confirmed transport plan and backup worker for the zone.

## Quality Standards

- Arrive in the scheduled time window.
- Capture before and after photos for every visit.
- Report issues immediately, not at the end of day.
- Keep tone respectful and formal with subscribers.
