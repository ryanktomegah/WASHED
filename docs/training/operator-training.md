# Operator Training

## Daily Start

- Open readiness tabs: payments, notifications, push providers, reconciliation.
- Review unmatched subscribers, active visits, worker issues, disputes, swaps, and payment failures.
- Confirm enough active workers cover each service cell.

## Matching

- Match by service cell first, then capacity, quality, availability, and route density.
- Review assignment decision logs when a candidate is rejected.
- Avoid assigning inactive workers or workers outside their service neighborhoods.

## Live Operations

- Watch the route/capacity map during morning visits.
- For no-show or cancellation, use live status controls and record the reason.
- For late or blocked visits, contact the worker first, then subscriber if necessary.

## Support

- Never expose worker or subscriber private phone numbers in product surfaces.
- For refunds, verify payment attempt, provider reference, visit, and dispute decision.
- For safety concerns, escalate immediately and suspend assignment if needed.

## End Of Day

- Confirm all completed visits have check-in, check-out, and photos.
- Review pending photo uploads and worker issues.
- Run payment reconciliation if provider webhooks were delayed.
