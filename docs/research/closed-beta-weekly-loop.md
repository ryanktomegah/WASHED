# Closed Beta Weekly Research Loop

## Cadence

Run interviews every week during closed beta:

- 5 active subscribers;
- 3 active workers;
- every subscriber or worker involved in a dispute;
- every churned subscriber within 72 hours.

Use the French scripts in `docs/research/subscriber-interview-script-fr.md` and `docs/research/worker-interview-script-fr.md`.

## Metrics Review

Before interviews, open the Ops Console Beta tab and record:

- visit completion rate;
- no-shows and cancellations;
- payment success rate;
- refunds and credits;
- open disputes;
- worker issues;
- worker payout failures;
- NPS and worker satisfaction survey averages when available.

## Weekly Decision Meeting

Every week, decide one of:

- keep operating unchanged;
- change route density or worker capacity;
- change price or tier packaging;
- change onboarding or support scripts;
- pause growth until a reliability issue is fixed.
