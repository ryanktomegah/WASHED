# Washed Banking Partner Diligence

**Purpose:** Compare banking partners before Washed handles paid subscriptions, worker payouts, refunds, settlement reconciliation, or operating reserves.

**Banks to compare:** Ecobank, Orabank, UTB, and any Togo/WAEMU alternative recommended by counsel, payment providers, or operators.

## Decision Needed

Select a primary operating bank and a fallback path for beta before taking paid subscriber funds.

## Required Capabilities

| Capability | Required for beta | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| Business account opening path | Yes | Must support the chosen legal entity and founder documentation. |
| Mobile-money settlement compatibility | Yes | Must receive provider settlements from T-Money/Flooz aggregator. |
| Statement exports | Yes | CSV/PDF exports needed for reconciliation and audit. |
| Low-friction transfers | Yes | Needed for vendors, refunds, and emergency payouts. |
| Clear fees | Yes | Monthly fees, transfer fees, account fees, and statement fees. |
| Multi-user controls | Preferred | Founder/operator access separation before scale. |
| WAEMU expansion support | Preferred | Helpful for Benin, Cote d'Ivoire, Senegal path. |
| Relationship manager | Preferred | Useful during payout or settlement incidents. |

## Questions for Each Bank

### Account Opening

- What entity documents are required?
- Can the founder begin preparation before incorporation is complete?
- What minimum deposit or monthly balance is required?
- How long does approval normally take?
- Are foreign-owner, director, or shareholder documents required?

### Settlement and Reconciliation

- Can the account receive settlements from CinetPay, PayDunya, or another mobile-money provider?
- What settlement references appear on statements?
- Can statements be exported by date range?
- Are intra-day statements available?
- Can the bank provide signed statements for audit, tax, or investor diligence?

### Transfers and Payout Operations

- What transfer rails are available for local vendors and worker-related payments?
- What limits apply per transfer and per day?
- What fees apply to local transfers, WAEMU transfers, and cash withdrawals?
- Can emergency transfers be approved quickly outside normal hours?
- Does the bank support dual approval for larger payments?

### Risk and Support

- What happens if a provider settlement is delayed or reversed?
- Who handles blocked transfers or compliance reviews?
- Is there an assigned relationship manager?
- What fraud monitoring or account freeze rules could affect beta operations?
- How are disputes documented?
