# Washed Trademark and Domain Checklist

**Purpose:** Prepare the brand, domain, and public-name checks needed before Washed spends on marketing, uniforms, public launch material, or paid domain assets.

**Status:** Operational checklist only. Final clearance must come from Togo/OAPI counsel or a qualified trademark agent.

## Decision Owner

- Founder: approve the name risk tolerance and fallback names.
- Counsel or trademark agent: confirm registrability and conflict risk.
- Operator: collect screenshots, search receipts, and domain availability evidence.

Track at least three candidates before committing public spend.

## Trademark Search Steps

1. Search exact and similar marks for laundry, cleaning, home services, mobile apps, marketplaces, transport, and payment-related services.
2. Check spelling variants: `Washed`, `Washd`, `WASHED`, `Washed TG`, `Washed Lome`.
3. Ask counsel to confirm relevant OAPI classes before filing. Likely areas to review include software/app services, laundry/cleaning services, marketplace services, and payment-adjacent service descriptions.
4. Save evidence: screenshots, search date, source, query used, and reviewer name.
5. Record conflict level: `clear`, `minor similarity`, `material conflict`, or `blocked`.

## Domain and Handle Checks

Run availability checks before buying:

```sh
whois washed.tg
whois washed.app
whois washed.africa
```

Also check:

- Google Play app name.
- Apple App Store app name.
- Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X, LinkedIn handles.
- WhatsApp Business display name availability.
- Email options such as `support@...`, `ops@...`, and `legal@...`.
