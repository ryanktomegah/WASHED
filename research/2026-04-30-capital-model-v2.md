# Washed Capital Model v2

**Date:** 2026-04-30
**FX assumption:** 1 USD = 605 XOF

This model separates closed-beta capital from public-launch runway and keeps unverified provider/legal quotes as explicit assumptions.

## Core Assumptions

- Subscriber ARPU: 3,300 XOF per month.
- Average visits: 1.4 per subscriber per month.
- Mobile-money fee placeholder: 2% of subscriber revenue.
- Worker model placeholder: 40,000 XOF floor plus 600 XOF per completed visit.
- Target steady-state capacity: about 25 subscribers per worker.
- App build is no longer modeled as one generic MVP line; it is replaced by hardening, review, tooling, and field operations reserves.
